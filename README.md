# Extransmutations

A collection of extra opus magnum glyphs, or extra transmutations, or extransmutations, if you will.
These are so far concerned with cardinals and are meant to be used in puzzles without berlo/duplication.
Currently includes:

### The Glyph of Cardinal Revolution:
The Glyph of Cardinal Revolution transmutes three matching atoms of the same cardinal into two salt atoms, and the next cardinal in the sequence Air -> Water -> Earth -> Fire -> Air (Clockwise along the chart of alchemical primes)

### The Glyph of Cardinal Inversion:
The Glyph of cardinal inversion transmutes two cardinals into an opposing cardinal and salt.

### The Glyph of Cardinal Completion:
The Glyph of Cardinal Completion transmutes 3 different cardinals and 1 salt atom into 4 atoms of the missing cardinal.
