# Extransmutations

A collection of extra opus magnum glyphs, or extra transmutations, or extransmutations, if you will.
These are so far concerned with cardinals and are meant to be used in puzzles without berlo/duplication.
Currently includes:

### The Glyph of Cardinal Revolution:
The Glyph of Cardinal Revolution transmutes three matching atoms of the same cardinal into two salt atoms, and the next cardinal in the sequence Air -> Water -> Earth -> Fire -> Air (Clockwise along the chart of alchemical primes)

### The Glyph of Cardinal Inversion:
The Glyph of cardinal inversion transmutes two cardinals into an opposing cardinal and salt.

### The Glyph of Cardinal Completion:
The Glyph of Cardinal Completion transmutes 3 different cardinals and 1 salt atom into 4 atoms of the missing cardinal.

### Glyph of Aeration
The Glyph of Aeration turns an atom of salt into Air.

### Glyph of Compaction
The Glyph of Compaction transmutes an atom of salt into an atom of Earth if it has 3 or more bonds. A full triplex counts as three bonds.

### Glyph of Liquidation
The Glyph of Liquidation transmutes an atom of quicksilver into lead in exchange for transmuting one OR two salt atoms into water.

### Glyph of Induction
The Glyph of Induction can attach its hollow hex to another glyph. Whenever a transmutation takes place in the attached hex, the glyph of induction will transmute an atom of salt into fire. It is not permitted to attach multiple induction hooks to the same hex, or to attach it to another glyph of induction.

## Ichor Mechanics
Ichor (new atom) suppresses outputs for as long as it is on the board. Get rid of it by using the Glyph of Recombination, Disposal, or by outputting it (which is allowed if it is the last atom to suppress output)

### Glyph of Dejection
The Glyph of Dejection transmutes a cardinal into an atom of Ichor and produces two cardinals one step backwards along the Cardinal Cycle (Fire -> x2 Earth -> x2 Water -> x2 Air -> x2 Fire)

### Glyph of Recombination
The Glyph of Recombination destroys two atoms of salt to turn an atom of Ichor back into salt.

### Glyph of Restoration
The Glyph of Restoration destroys all atoms of ichor present in the molecule over the ichor bowl and all atoms of the cardinal over the cardinal bowl when the number of ichor atoms and the number of said cardinal atoms present is exactly the same.
